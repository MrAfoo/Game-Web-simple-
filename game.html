Featured Game

Agar.io

agar.io is a multiplayer online game where players control a cell, consuming smaller cells to grow while avoiding being eaten by larger ones. The game features simple mechanics and strategic gameplay as players navigate a vast petri dish, competing for dominance!

Agma.io

agma.io is an evolution of agar.io, offering enhanced gameplay with various modes, skins, and challenges. Players grow their cells by consuming others while utilizing unique abilities and strategies in a colorful, interactive environment!

Snake.io

snake.io is a fun multiplayer game where players control a snake, consuming food to grow longer while avoiding collisions with other snakes. The goal is to outmaneuver opponents and become the largest snake on the map in this fast-paced, competitive setting.
