Featured Game

FREE FIRE

Free Fire is a fast-paced battle royale game where players parachute onto an island and compete to be the last one standing. Matches last about 10 minutes, featuring a variety of characters, weapons, and strategic elements!

PUBG

PUBG (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds) is a battle royale game where up to 100 players parachute onto an island and fight to be the last alive. Players scavenge for weapons and gear while navigating a shrinking play zone.

VALORANT

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter where teams of five players choose Agents with unique abilities to secure victory. With a focus on strategy, communication, and precise gunplay, each match is a test of teamwork and skill!
